About Us

Phoenix is the annual technical festival of the Computer Society of India(CSI)-DTU Chapter.It is a four day long series of events and activities that include Coding Events, Gaming ,Quizzing, Code Breaking and several other informal events that will take your idea of computing to a whole new level and lets you explore your creative and technical side in a way you have never done before. The name “Phoenix” is symbolic of rebirth and revival of the dauntless spirit and never subsiding enthusiasm of the people working behind this event. After the magnificent success of its previous installment, Phoenix 2015 is one of the most eagerly awaited techfest among the engineering youth of the country.
